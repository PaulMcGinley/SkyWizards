About Legend of Sky Wizards

Welcome to Legend of Sky Wizards, a game series that brings fantasy and adventure to life across two unique formats: a 3D game built in Unreal Engine and a 2D game created with SFML. These games are part of my second-year college course, where I aim to develop not just gameplay experiences but also a foundation of skills in both 3D and 2D development.

I’ve crafted these games using freely available, no-cost assets, which have been invaluable in making my vision possible without the need for costly resources. This open-access approach has inspired me to give back in the same way, providing the source code, tools, and resources I’ve developed along the journey. I believe in making learning accessible, so everything I share comes with no cost or obligation—just as the assets that brought these games to life were shared with me.

Building a Portfolio

In addition to creating an enjoyable game series, I’m building Legend of Sky Wizards as a showcase of my skills as a software developer. These projects demonstrate not only my dedication to game development but also my versatility in software engineering, from managing resources and assets to developing tools and creating an interconnected ecosystem of features. I hope to use this series as a key portfolio piece as I look to advance my career in software development.

Open Development

Tools for Creators

As part of my commitment to open development, I will be releasing the tools I created to support the 2D game’s development, allowing others to add custom content, levels, and features to the game. I want players and creators to expand the Legend of Sky Wizards world, building on the foundation I’ve laid and crafting their own experiences within it. These tools will be freely available for anyone who wants to explore, customize, or even extend the game’s possibilities.

Share and Download User-Created Content

To further empower the community, user-created content will be shareable through these tools, hosted on my servers, and directly downloadable within the game. This means that custom levels and features developed by the community will be accessible to everyone, enriching the experience for all players and fostering a creative ecosystem within Legend of Sky Wizards.

Contact

Coffee and a chat?

If you would like to get in touch with me regarding my projects, or just to chat about games, please feel free to drop me a message. If you are a creator of an asset I am using and would like me to remove it from the projects or make a correction to how I use and distribute it, please let me know.
