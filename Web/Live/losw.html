The Legend of Sky Wizards

A 3D platformer set in a broken world.

In Legend of Sky Wizards, players enter a world shattered by a mysterious and powerful entity who, in a fit of cosmic boredom, has splintered the wizard’s planet, leaving fragments of land suspended in the sky. The wizards, now bound to these floating islands, must prove their resilience by navigating a series of challenging obstacle courses and solving puzzles with their magic.

But beware – this playful but capricious entity is watching, and failure won’t be the end. Death is only a setback, as the entity won’t let the wizards escape the game that easily. Respawn, strategize, and keep moving forward in this enchanting test of agility and wit. Will you master the art of sky-bound survival, or will the entity’s game outwit you?

Legend of Sky Wizards is a unique game concept developed as part of a college assignment for my class 'Game Customisation and Scripting'. This project challenged us to design and implement a customizable, interactive experience with scripted mechanics, focusing on creative use of game elements like character movement, environmental obstacles, and player progression.

The game *play centers around wizards navigating a fragmented, floating world, forced to complete parkour-style challenges by a powerful entity.
